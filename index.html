Water Pollution

Water pollution refers to the contamination of oceans, seas, rivers, and other water bodies by harmful substances. It includes plastic waste, oil spills, industrial chemicals, agricultural runoff, and untreated sewage. These pollutants disrupt marine ecosystems and threaten human health.

Sonar Types

Sonar (Sound Navigation and Ranging) uses sound waves to detect and locate objects underwater. It is used in marine research, navigation, and defense. Active sonar emits pulses; passive sonar listens only.
